My trading technique begins with the analysis of the major timeframes with increasing resolution; Weekly, Daily, 4Hr, 1Hr, 15Mins, 5Mins, using indicators, oscillators and wave charting to establish confluence and find trends.

Particularly, usage of a double MACD with signal colours and signal line is used in conjunction RSI to understand overbought/sold events alongside trend reversal. If confluence holds with the Elliott Waves and fib levels, then a potential trade is positioned.

The trades focus on attaining profits over a few days to a few weeks using the Daily, 4Hr, and 1Hr charts. Stop losses provide some security, with TPs usually at 50% or 61.8% fib. Some, if not all profit is taken at this point, with potential trailing profits upon re-analysis of the momentum. For me, it is better to confirm some/all profit than bet on another impulse.
